Bāng-tiong ê chêng-ōe, a--sī chin iah sī ké
M̄-kóan hong-hō͘ chóaⁿ-iūⁿ tī chhoe, gún iû-gôan m̄-kam chheng-chhéⁿ
Bāng-tiong teng-lêng ê ōe, sui-jiân chí-ū kuí kù ōe
Hō͘ góa un-lóan chāi sim-té, chin-siūⁿ beh lâi iōng-sim kau-pôe
Bāng-tiong ê chêng-ōe, a--sī chin m̄-sī ké
Sui-jiân bāng-kéng hiah-ní té, ài lí éng-óan bōe hóan-hóe
Bāng-tiong teng-lêng ê ōe, san-bêng-hái-sè chi̍t-kù-ōe
Bāng lí kín-kì chāi sim-té, góa ē iōng chin-sim lâi kau-pôe
Kám-siā lí tuì góa chiah thán-pe̍h, hō͘ góa ū tiông-sin chhut-hoat ê ki-hōe
Chho͘-lôan ê chu-bī iá chhì-kòe, tio̍h hō͘ kám-chêng lâi léng-chēng thiat-té
Chí-iau lán siōng-ài kòe-têng bí-lē
Tō suî iân-hūn an-pâi it-chhè
Kám-siā thiⁿ ê sêng-chôan thé-hōe, hō͘-lán ū chi̍t-pái
Siong-ài ê ki-hōe
