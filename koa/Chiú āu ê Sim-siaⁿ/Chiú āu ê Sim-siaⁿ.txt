San-bêng-hái-sè, lán nn̄g-lâng ū chiù-chōa
Uī chóaⁿ-iūⁿ lí, phian-phian lâi piàn-kòa
Góa siūⁿ bōe-hiáu, lí ná ē chiah hi-hoa
Khi-phiàn liáu góa, chhì-kek tio̍h góa

Chio̍h-thâu ē nōa, chhiáⁿ lí ài siong-sìn góa
Chòe-āu ê kiat-kó, iā-sī bô-khah-choa̍h
Gêng-sim m̄-kiaⁿ chiú kāu, hiông-hiông chi̍t-chhuì lim hō͘ ta
Siōng-hó chuì sí, mài koh oa̍h

Ah, góa bô chuì, góa bô chuì, bô chuì
Chhiáⁿ lí m̄-bián tông-chêng góa
Chiú nā lo̍h-âu, thiàⁿ ji̍p sim-koaⁿ
Siong-sim ê siong-sim ê góa
Sim-chêng bô-lâng ē chai-iáⁿ
Chí-ū sio-chiú liáu-kái góa
