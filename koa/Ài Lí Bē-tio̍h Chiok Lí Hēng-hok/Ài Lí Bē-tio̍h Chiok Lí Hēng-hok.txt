Bêng-bêng lí sī góa ûi-it ê sim-lāi
Hiān-si̍t ê seng-oa̍h

Pek lán hun tang-sai, mn̄g thiⁿ thiⁿ mn̄g tē
Chi̍t-chióng thòng-khó͘, siáⁿ-lâng chai

M̄ siūⁿ lí chin chin chin, chin siong-pi
Sui-jiân chhim-chêng pàng bōe-lī
Mā tio̍h chhōe chi̍t-ê lí-iû, an-ùi góa ka-kī
